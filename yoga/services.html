Take an Insight into the Spirituality of Simple Yoga

Home » Our Services

In the words of Bhagavad Gita, “Yoga is a journey of the self, through the self, to the self”, and that’s the divine journey of spiritual learning and aligning body, mind, and energy that we take you on.
Simple Yoga is an initiative conceptualized to create a comprehensive culture towards providing spiritual solutions for highest possibility of potential, expressions, and perception while staying in harmony with your inner and outer environment. At every stage of imparting the best training of energy activation, meditation, postures, and breathing exercises, we give an insight into the depths and dimensions of yoga. In the process, you tend to discover your true self with an increased clarity and control of your thoughts and emotions. You do not move away from reality and neither do you shed down your responsibilities; rather you get the energy, the motivation, and the firmness of accepting life as it comes.
Underlying the health benefits of yoga, we provide various thoughtful programs and training sessions in Mumbai. You can learn and practice yoga with simple techniques like simply breathing, singly stretching, silently meditating and other simplified asnas through our learned and experienced yoga teachers and practitioners.
