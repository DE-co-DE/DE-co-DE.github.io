Welcome To Yoga Classes

In this age of studio yoga, power yoga, sound yoga, here come the concept of Simple Yoga. You would learn and practise yoga in a very simplified way and still derive the same kind and quantum of benefits, when it is pursued in its larger form.
When you realise yourself and when the mind is organized, the communication channel between you and your source of creation opens up, then you would get whatever you wish for. As a consequence, everything else will get organized automatically. We teach you how to realise your higher possibility When it comes to defining this aspect of science, it displays a philosophy that imparts learning and understanding of how you can go beyond the physical aspects of life through exercises and breathing activities. If you go by the Vedas, it helps to remove all the layers of illusions and expectations while erasing all negative thoughts, betrayals, heartaches, fears, anxiety, and all such demons from the inner walls of your existence. This in turn leaves you body pure and peaceful giving way for an immortal spirit to reside, a clean body for a pure soul. That is the beauty and essence of ‘Yoga’.
